About

Nothing says summer like a great movie night! Whether you're at the drive-in, on the couch, or under the stars, these films bring the fun, adventure, and nostalgia of summer to life. Grab your popcorn and let's dive in!

Jaws (1975)

The ultimate summer thriller! Spielberg's classic about a great white shark terrorizing a beach town is a must-watch for suspense and sun-soaked chills.

The Sandlot (1993)

Baseball, friendship, and endless summer days. This coming-of-age comedy is pure nostalgia and perfect for a feel-good movie night.

Mamma Mia! (2008)

Set on a sun-drenched Greek island, this musical is packed with ABBA hits, romance, and summer vibes. Don't forget to sing along!
