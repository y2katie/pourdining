Bar & Lounge

The bar at POUR Dining is the party you'll want to return to night after night.
Our cocktail menu highlights a mix of NYC classics and innovative newcomers, alongside our deep collection of international wines and spirits featuring producers from around the globe.

Signature Cocktails & Spirits

Our award-winning bar program features a curated selection of classic and contemporary cocktails, rare spirits, and a wine list recognized among the best in New York. Whether you're seeking a perfectly balanced Manhattan, a creative seasonal spritz, or a glass of Champagne to toast the skyline, our bartenders are ready to craft your perfect drink.

Atmosphere & Experience

Our lounge offers panoramic city views, plush seating, and a vibrant yet relaxed ambiance. Whether you're meeting friends for after-work drinks, enjoying a romantic date, or celebrating a special occasion, the Bar & Lounge at POUR Dining is the perfect destination.

Walk-ins are welcome! Reservations are not required for the bar and lounge, but are recommended for large groups and special occasions.
